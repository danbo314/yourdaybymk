About MK

When I entered my senior year at Notre Dame and excitedly spoke about opening my own wedding planning firm, everyone thought I was joking. I was a political science major who played club ultimate frisbee instead of learning about stock market trends. But here I am living my dream while many of my friends have switched jobs multiple times or are on completely different life paths, years out of school. Nothing beats knowing I’m doing what I love everyday. This means I truly love answering your emails, speaking with multiple caterers, venues and bands all to make your special day AMAZING! Whether it’s an intimate bridal shower, philanthropic gala or a large family wedding, being stressed about the little things should not be on your radar. I thrive off of daily human interaction and love the thrill of problem solving during crunch time. But seeing the smile on your face when you realize it is finally the day you’ve been waiting for, that’s why I do what I do. Every detail and last minute phone call is worth it for a lifetime of memories. So, let’s grab coffee and gush about everything and anything.

MK was incredible doing the logistics for our group trip to Cincinnati. She handled nearly every aspect of the organization, from paperwork and forms to obtaining a great block hotel rate so group could stay together. Knowing we could count on her to take care of all the details was amazing, and it freed us up to think about other aspects of our trip.
